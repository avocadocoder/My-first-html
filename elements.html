Caroline Chisholm College

90-98 The Lakes Drive, Glenmore Park, NSW

Students Enrolled

The school has around 1000 or more students. It is a girls school from years 7-12 estimating 100-200 students per year group.

Courses offered

The school offers a range of subjects including English, Mathematics, Science, History, Geography, Art, Music, and Physical Education. For year 11 and 12 electives, one subject branches to more, such as science becoming into biology, physics and chemistry. Technology branches into Food Tech, Agricultural Tech, Digital Tech and Design Tech.

This school is registered with the NSW Education Standards Authority (NESA). It operates as a catholic systemic school within the Diocese of Parramatta.
