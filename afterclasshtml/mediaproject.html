Diljit Dosanjh

Diljit Dosanjh is a popular Punjabi musical artist. He often collaborates with other popular artists such as; Ed Sheeran and Sia. He is my favourite punjabi singer and last year I was able to go to his concert in Sydney. He was also the first Punjabi celebrity to be invited to the Met Gala, which was in 2025.
